Est. 2026 · Lahore, Pakistan

Born from a
Simple Belief.

Atif wasn't trying to build a brand.He was just tired. Tired of buying bags that looked beautiful in the store and fell apart within months. Handles that snapped. Zips that jammed. Money spent, and nothing to show for it.Walking through the markets of Lahore one afternoon, he stopped at a woven basket. Old. Worn. But completely intact. He turned it over in his hands and thought why don't we make bags like this anymore?That question became Wovenaa..

The 4,000 Year Old Answer

He went deeper. And what he found stopped him.In 2000 BC, Ancient Egyptians were already weaving reeds, grass, and natural fibers into bags. No factories. No machines. Just a technique so strong, it carried an entire civilisation's world and survived four thousand years to tell the story.The weave wasn't a trend. It was never a trend.It was the answer humans had already figured out  long before we forgot it.

Why We Built This

We live in a world of use and throw. Buy it today. Bin it by next season. And somehow, we've accepted that as normal.Wovenaa was built for the people who haven't.For the woman who buys once and keeps forever. Who wants handles that don't snap under real weight. A bag that doesn't ask to be replaced every summer. Something that carries not just her things  but her story.Every Wovenaa piece is woven to last. Not for a season. For years. This is not a purchase. It is a decision.

Today

From Lahore, we ship across Pakistan  and we are just getting started.Every Wovenaa piece leaves us with a personal note inside. Because we believe the person carrying it deserves to know what they are holding a craft tested by 4,000 years of human history.Ancient craft. Modern strength. Built for permanence.

Care Guide

Caring for Your
Wovenaa.

Natural materials deserve natural care. Follow these four steps to keep your piece beautiful for years to come.

01

Keep It Dry

Keep your Wovenaa away from rain and moisture. If it does get wet, gently pat dry with a soft cloth and leave it in open air never in direct sunlight or near a heater. Allow it to dry completely before storing

02

Clean Gently

For the straw body, wipe with a lightly damp cloth. Never scrub. For vegan leather trim, wipe gently with a dry cloth only. Avoid chemical cleaners or alcohol-based products on any part of the bag.

03

Store With Care

Always store your Wovenaa in the ivory dust bag provided. Keep it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, which can fade the natural straw colour over time. Stuff the bag lightly with tissue paper to help it hold its shape when not in use.

04

Carry With Care

Wovenaa bags are woven to carry your world but carry it with intention. Avoid overstuffing, as this can strain the natural weave. Be mindful of sharp objects inside the bag. The leather handles will develop a beautiful patina with use this is a feature, not a flaw.
